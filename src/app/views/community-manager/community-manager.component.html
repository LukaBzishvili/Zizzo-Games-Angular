Community Manager

Location: San Francisco

Employment Type: Contract

What You'll Do

As a community manager, my days would be filled with building connections, fostering engagement, and nurturing a thriving community of passionate players. I would embark on a journey where I get to be the bridge between the game development team and the players, ensuring open communication, addressing concerns, and cultivating a positive and inclusive environment.

Who You Are

Building strong relationships with the community would be at the core of my work. By actively engaging with players, I would foster a sense of belonging and create spaces where they can connect, share experiences, and contribute to the growth and improvement of the game. I would celebrate their achievements, amplify their voices, and make them feel valued as integral members of the gaming community.
